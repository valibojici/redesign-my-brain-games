Mixed signals test

You will hear a voice saying a number between 1 and 7 and shortly after you will see symbols on the screen. They may be digits, letters or '%', '$', '#', '@'. You must press the spacebar when the number you hear is the same as the number of items on screen. If the number is not the same you must wait. Initial reaction time window (the time you have to press the spacebar if you have to) is 1400ms. Default number of rounds is 15. Change them with the sliders below.
